Keller, Texas

Big-City Convenience with Small-Town Charm

Located in the heart of the Dallas-Fort Worth Metroplex, Keller, Texas is a city that offers the best of both worlds. On one hand, it provides the conveniences and amenities of a big city while on the other, it boasts a small-town feel with an emphasis on quality of life. The city is home to approximately 46,000 residents who live within its 18.4 square miles.

One of the reasons for Keller's popularity is its excellent school system, which has helped make it one of the best places to raise a family in Texas. In addition, the city has a thriving economy, with two distinct business districts, Old Town Keller and the urban Keller Town Center, which offer residents a wide range of shopping and dining options.

The city is also a great place for outdoor enthusiasts. The citywide parks and trails system, anchored by Big Bear Creek, provides plenty of opportunities for recreation, and the Keller Pointe, the city’s recreation and aquatics center, is a great place to stay active and healthy. For those who prefer indoor activities, the Keller Public Library offers a large collection of books and online resources, while the new Keller Senior Activities Center provides seniors with a place to come together for socialization and stimulating activities.

In recent years, Keller has been recognized as one of the "Best Small Cities in America," one of the "Top 10 Places to Live in Texas," and one of the "Best Places to Raise a Family in Texas." With a low crime rate, thanks to its accredited police and fire departments, Keller is also consistently named one of the safest cities in the country.

In conclusion, if you're looking for a city that offers a high quality of life, a strong sense of community, and plenty of opportunities for recreation and entertainment, Keller is definitely worth considering. With its proximity to both Fort Worth and Dallas and the DFW Airport, it's the perfect place to call home.
