Mark Hazleton - Solutions Architect

Mark Hazleton is a co-founder of Control Origins, a leading provider of technology solutions for economic analysis. He is a seasoned entrepreneur with a passion for technology and economics, and his expertise is a valuable asset to TexEcon.com.

With his background in both technology and economics, Mark brings a unique perspective to the world of economic analysis. He is committed to using technology to help people understand the complexities of the economy and to make the analysis of economic data more accessible and understandable.

At Control Origins, Mark has helped to develop cutting-edge solutions for the analysis of economic data and has been recognized as a leader in the field. His insights and expertise are widely respected, and he is a sought-after speaker at conferences and events.

In addition to his work at Control Origins, Mark is also a regular contributor to TexEcon.com, where he provides technical insights and commentary on the use of technology in economic analysis. His expertise and insights are an important addition to our platform, and we are proud to have him as a member of our team.

Join us in welcoming Mark Hazleton to TexEcon.com, and be sure to check out his contributions to our platform. With his combination of technology and economic expertise, Mark is sure to provide valuable insights and perspectives on the Texas economy.
